In Remembrance

John Doe

2020-2020

The purpose of this website would be to keep this person close to people's hearts, not to prolong the sufferring, but to help accept the present and trust that he or she has gone to a better, happier place.

A flower would hold a message that someone wrote about this person. It can be one of their fond memories of the person that has passed or anything nice that they want to share.

Need assistance on how to interact with this website?

Doing multiple clicks on the person will toggle their skin color and change whether the name appears.

Clicking on the picture frame will change whether the name appears.

Selecting the flower will show messages.
